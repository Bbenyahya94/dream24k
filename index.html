Yes. Dream24K is the rebrand of Dream4K. Same team, same servers, now with more channels and upgraded 4K infrastructure.
How much does it cost?
Subscription plans: 1 month = 10€, 3 months = 20€, 6 months = 30€, 12 months = 40€. Reseller packages available from 140€.
What devices are supported?
Smart TVs, Firestick, Android, iOS, MAG/STB boxes, Windows, Mac. Any device with IPTV Smarters, TiviMate, or similar.

No. Dream24K works without a VPN in most regions. VPN is optional for added privacy.
What payment methods are accepted?
Visa, Mastercard, American Express, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and cryptocurrency (Bitcoin, USDT, Ethereum).
What is your refund policy?
Every subscription is backed by a 30-day money-back guarantee. Contact our support team for a full refund.

Activation is instant. You'll receive your credentials via email within 60 seconds of completing your purchase.
